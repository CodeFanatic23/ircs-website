The OBJECTIVES


  1. Providing Safe Blood & Blood products consistently through regular healthy voluntary non-remunerated blood donors to the patients.

  2. To reduce the risk of Transfusion transmitted diseases as the Voluntary Blood Donors being healthy representatives of the society and are safest Blood Donors as they are from low-risk groups.

  3. Voluntary Donors are regular Blood Donors so the gap between availability & the demand of blood in the country can be reduced.

  4. Provide safe and quality blood and blood components collected from voluntary donors, round the clock, at affordable cost to the general public and free of cost to the poor.

  5. Ensure safety and quality of blood.

  6. Motivate and maintain a permanent well-indexed record of voluntary blood donors.

  7. Educating the community on the beneficial aspects of blood donation and harmful effect of collecting blood from paid donors.

  8. Actively encourage voluntary blood donation and gradually eliminate professional/paid blood donors.

  9. Promote AIDS awareness and education to the general public.

  10. Assists the various Organizations, Clubs, Colleges, Public & Private Institutions and the Public to conduct voluntary blood donation drives and arrange for motivational talks to enable progressively increase the number of voluntary non-remunerated blood donors every year.

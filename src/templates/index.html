About Us

Indian Red Cross Society has been running the largest Voluntary Blood Banks in India since 1962 and at present contributing 10% of the total blood collection through its 110 blood bank centers across the country. Blood and Blood products are issued free of cost to the Thalassemic and Haemophilic patients and to Government hospitals.
